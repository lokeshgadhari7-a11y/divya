Overview

Our platform integrates IoT sensors, cloud-based analytics and remote dashboards to help biogas plants monitor performance, detect issues early and optimize output. By measuring parameters such as gas flow, pressure, temperature, composition and system health, you gain full transparency and control.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sensors are included in your system?
We include flow meters, gas composition sensors (methane, CO₂, H₂S), temperature, pressure, and optional pH/ORP sensors depending on plant type.
Can we integrate with existing digesters?
Yes — our solution supports retrofitting of legacy plants. We provide modular hardware and cloud configuration for existing setups.
How is the data accessed and secured?
Data is transmitted securely to cloud servers via encrypted channels. Users access dashboards via login. Role-based permissions are supported.
Is remote support available?
Yes — we provide remote monitoring, alerting and diagnostics. For enterprise plans, on-site support is available.
